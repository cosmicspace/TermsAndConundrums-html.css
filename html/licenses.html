Did you know?...

...you can write your own license? Often, licenses are distributed with the ability to modify them. Of course, this makes your new license somewhat different from the original, but it gives you a great deal of power in deciding what is important to you.

You should consult an attorney since you might be surprised what can happen when you change a license. For example, if you allow completely open rights for everyone, someone could take a copy of your work and claim it as their own creation, copyright or patent it and sue you for copyright or patent infringement for using it, even though it was your creation!
